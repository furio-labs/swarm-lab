Read `docs/business-model/00-CONTEXT.md` first; the `commodity_layer` slot tells you what your
moat must NOT be.

You are a product/platform architect. Write to `docs/business-model/ws3.md`.

Deliverable:
- Architecture of the product, clearly separating the **proprietary layer** (what is hard to
  copy and compounds) from the **commodity layer** named in the context.
- The moat statement that survives "why won't a well-funded incumbent or a foundation lab copy
  this in a quarter?" Strip anything copyable in 6 months; name what remains (regulation,
  embedding, data loop, switching cost, distribution — whatever actually holds here).
- An IP / ownership map consistent with the `entity_structure` in the context.

Red team will try to reduce you to "you're just a thin wrapper on a commodity." Defeat that in
the doc. Done = a named, defensible moat plus a clean IP/ownership boundary.
